The word "Kopitara" can be a combination of the word "Kopi" (coffee) and exotic or mystical elements, so it can create an impression of magic and adventure in serving coffee. You can also serve various types of coffee from various parts of the world, so that customers feel like they are exploring the world through the taste of the coffee served. Apart from that, "Kopitara Cafe" can offer a relaxed and friendly atmosphere, where customers can feel like they are on a coffee journey full of mystery, beauty and enjoyment.
